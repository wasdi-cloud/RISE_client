About Us

The RISE Web App brings the power of satellite intelligence to your fingertips. Access near real-time data, visualize environmental changes, and generate instant reports, all in one intuitive platform designed to help you make smarter, faster decisions.

Mission And Vision

RISE is a product developed by WASDI Sarl. WASDI S.a.r.l. is a joint venture formed in December 2020 between FadeOut Software (a software development SME in Italy), and the Luxembourg Institute of Science and Technology.

WASDI envisions a world where remote sensing, technology and science empower communities to collaborate and build resilience against climate disasters, ensuring sustainability and worldwide protection with a special focus for the most vulnerable.

RISE exists to transform state-of-the-art scientific research into accessible, operational services, empowering decision-makers and frontline responders with actionable satellite data for environmental monitoring, humanitarian aid, and sustainable development.

WASDI

RISE has been developed by WASDI Sarl in the context of the Humanitarian Innovation Action, promoted by the World Food Programme Innovation Accelerator (HIIA). RISE automatically processes satellite data into clear hazard maps, helping responders quickly assess floods, droughts, and other risks, while also using historical archives to track impacts over time.

LIST contributed to RISE developing the algorithms for Flood Detection in bare soil and in urban areas, to detect the buildings and to evaluate the evapotranspiration to evaluate drought stress.

The Luxembourg Red Cross, Shelter Research Unit, has been involved in the project to define the use cases and to test the solution on the field, in Madagascar. (See Impact stories)

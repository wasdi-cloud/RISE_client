RISE Platform

The RISE Web App brings the power of satellite intelligence to your fingertips. Access near real-time data, visualize environmental changes, and generate instant reports, all in one intuitive platform designed to help you make smarter, faster decisions.

Get Started with RISE in 4 Simple Steps:

Step 1

Register & Invite Your Team

  • Sign up and create your organization's account.
  • Invite colleagues and assign roles (e.g., Admin, Analyst, Viewer) to manage access.

Step 2

Set Up Your Monitoring Area

  • Define the geographic area you want to monitor.
  • Select plugins (e.g., Floods, Droughts) to activate for your needs.

Step 3

Receive Real-Time Updates

  • RISE processes satellite imagery and publishes updated maps automatically.
  • Get a quick overview of all areas and active events in your dashboard. Select plugins (e.g., Floods, Droughts) to activate for your needs.

Step 4

Explore & Analyze

  • Click on an area to open the monitoring view.
  • Toggle maps on/off, browse historical data, and track changes over time.
  • Evaluate impacts with visual maps and chart-based analytics.

Stay Connected Anywhere

Internet issues? Use the RISE Telegram Bot for updates in your chat.
No connectivity? Print maps for offline use in the field.

Critical information is always within reach!

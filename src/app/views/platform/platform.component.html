RISE Platform

The RISE Web App brings the power of satellite intelligence to your fingertips. Access near real-time data, visualize environmental changes, and generate instant reports, all in one intuitive platform designed to help you make smarter, faster decisions.

Get Started with RISE in 4 Simple Steps:

Step 1

Register Your Team

Create your organization account. Use Admin, Analyst, Viewer roles to manage your team.

Step 2

Set Up Your Monitoring Area

Define the geographic areas your organization needs to monitor. RISE will configure the right plugins and data sources for your needs.

Step 3

Receive Near Real-Time Updates

RISE processes satellite data automatically. Get a daily overview of all your plugin (e.g., floods, droughts) for each area.

Step 4

Explore & Analyse

Click on an area to open an interactive map. Explore RISE data, export reports, analyse changes over time.

Stay Connected Anywhere

Download and use the RISE Telegram Bot to connect the RISE platform to your messaging app. Get RISE Telegram updates for your area via Telegram so that Critical information is always within reach!

Discover RISE Plugins in Details

Customize RISE with specialized plugins tailored to your needs:

Flood

  • Flood Monitoring: Near real-time mapping of open floods in urban and rural areas.
  • Flood Archive: Access historical flood data via an interactive timeline for trend analysis, short-events monitoring.
  • Flood-prone areas: Identify high-risk areas showing flood frequency and vulnerability.

Droughts

  • Early warning system for droughts.
  • Drought Risk Monitoring.

Buildings

  • Near real-time monitoring of your area and cities.

Impacts

  • Data migration and recovery effort (e.g. assessing the effects of natural disaster on key assets).
  • Population: Indicates the number of affected people, their locations, and age-density, ink zones.
  • Critical infrastructure: Identify new or affected essential facilities.
  • Crops: Review agriculture status vulnerable to or affected by disasters, supporting food security assessments.

Rain

  • Get Near Real-Time Rainfall Measurements.
  • Up to 91 and 24-hour Cumulative Rainfall Observation.

Urban Mapping

  • Near real-time mapping of your area and cities.
  • Detect and map urban zones to assist urban planning, disaster preparedness, and resilience efforts.

Wildfires

  • Early detection of wildfires that may be real (Coming Soon!).
  • Mapping of active wildfires.
  • Burned area mapping.

RISE Platform

RISE delivers satellite intelligence at your fingertips. Access near real-time data, visualize environmental changes, and generate instant reports, all in one intuitive platform designed to help you make smarter, faster decisions.

Get Started in 4 Simple Steps:

Step 1

Register and Invite Your Team

  • Sign up and create your organization's account.
  • Invite colleagues and assign roles (e.g., Admin, Analyst, Viewer) to manage access.

Step 2

Set Up Your Monitoring Area

  • Define the geographical area you want to monitor.
  • Select the plugins (e.g., Floods, Droughts) to activate for your needs.

Step 3

Receive Real-Time Updates

  • RISE processes satellite imagery and automatically publishes updated maps.
  • Get a quick overview of all areas and active events on your dashboard.

Step 4

Explore and Analyze

  • Click into an area to open the monitoring view.
  • Toggle maps, browse historical data, and track changes over time.
  • Evaluate impacts with visual maps and chart-based analytics.

Stay Connected Anywhere

Internet issues? Use the RISE Telegram Bot for updates in your chat.
No connectivity? Print maps for offline use in the field.

Discover RISE Plugins in Detail

Customize RISE with specialized plugins tailored to your needs: Have a glimpse on what the platform can do.

Flood

Monitor water over your area of interest:

  • Flood Monitoring: Near real-time mapping of floods in open and urban areas for rapid response.
  • Flood Archive: Access historical flood data via an interactive time-bar for deep review and analysis.
  • Flood Depth: Visualize flood water depth to assess severity and prioritize actions.
  • Flood Prone Areas: Identify high-risk areas by showing frequency and vulnerability to flooding.

Drought

Track vegetation health over time:

  • Drought Monitoring: Continuous assessment of vegetation health and surface conditions to detect drought patterns.
  • VCI and VHI Indices: Analyze Vegetation Condition Index (VCI) and Vegetation Health Index (VHI) for agricultural insights.
  • Precipitation Anomalies: Compare current rainfall with historical averages to identify water stress.

Buildings

Detailed analysis of structure and infrastructure:

  • Building Footprints: High-precision mapping of built-up areas for urban planning and disaster impact analysis.
  • Infrastructure Exposure: Identify critical buildings (e.g., hospitals, schools) in potential hazard zones.
  • Urban Growth: Track temporal changes in building density to monitor rapid urbanization.

Impacts

Understand the human and environmental cost:

  • Instantly calculate the extent of damage to buildings, agriculture, and infrastructure.
  • Population Affected: Estimate the number of people impacted within a disaster zone.
  • Infrastructure Exposure: Identify critical buildings (e.g., hospitals, schools) in potential hazard zones.
  • Damage Reports: Generate automated summaries of impacted assets for rapid situational reporting.

What is Rise? Instant Insights From Satellite Data
RISE is a cloud-based platform that provides near real-time monitoring of natural hazards worldwide, combining Earth Observation (EO) data and advanced analytics to help detect, analyze, and respond to disasters faster.
Who is RISE for? For Organizations That Need Results
NGOs, environmental agencies, agribusinesses, and insurers rely on RISE to respond faster, track changes, and secure supply chains, all without middlemen or delays.

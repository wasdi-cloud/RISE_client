RISE is a cloud‑based platform that integrates Earth Observation (EO) data with advanced analytics to deliver near real‑time intelligence on natural hazards worldwide. It provides the technology organizations need to anticipate risks and strengthen resilience.
What is Rise? Science‑driven innovation for disaster resilience
Built in collaboration with the WFP Innovation Accelerator, LIST, and the Luxembourg Red Cross, RISE transforms cutting‑edge geospatial technology into decision‑ready intelligence. helping organizations transform complex hazard data into clear, decision‑ready insights that drive smarter action.
Who is RISE for? Tailored to those who safeguard communities and manage risk
RISE is built for NGOs, environmental agencies, agribusinesses, and insurance companies that need to respond to disasters, track environmental changes, or secure supply chains. By eliminating intermediaries, it delivers scalable, cloud-based solutions tailored to operational needs.
Why choose RISE? Faster. Smarter. More Effective.
With RISE, organizations gain real-time monitoring, field-tested accuracy, and seamless integration into existing workflows. It’s designed to accelerate response times, optimize resources, and maximize impact, empowering teams to make smarter decisions under pressure.

Real Results, Real Impact
RISE gave the Luxembourg Red Cross and Malagasy teams a powerful advantage during Cyclone Cheneso. By combining historical archives with near real-time imagery, teams assessed flood severity, identified vulnerable populations, and evaluated infrastructure damage faster and more accurately than traditional methods, demonstrating the transformative potential of actionable geospatial intelligence.

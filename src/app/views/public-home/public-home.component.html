RISE is a cloud-based platform that integrates Earth Observation (EO) data with advanced analytics to provide near real-time intelligence on natural hazards worldwide. It provides the technology organizations need to anticipate risks and strengthen resilience.
What is RISE?
Built in collaboration with the WFP Innovation Accelerator, the LIST and the Luxembourg Red Cross, RISE transforms cutting-edge geospatial technology into decision-ready intelligence. helping organizations turn complex hazard data into clear, decision-ready insights that guide smarter actions.
Who is RISE for?
RISE is built for NGOs, environmental agencies, agribusinesses, and insurance companies who need to respond to disasters, track environmental changes, or secure supply chains. By eliminating the middleman, it provides scalable, cloud-based solutions tailored to operational needs.
Why choose RISE?
With RISE, organizations gain real-time monitoring, field-tested accuracy, and seamless integration into existing workflows. It’s designed to accelerate response times, optimize resources, and maximize impact, empowering teams to make smarter decisions under pressure.

Discover RISE Features
Discover how RISE empowers your organization with advanced tools for smarter, faster decision-making:
Near Real-Time Monitoring
RISE keeps you ahead of change with near real-time geospatial and satellite information. By providing continuous situational awareness, the platform empowers teams to make decisions and act quickly, reduce risks, and respond proactively to emergencies as soon as they occur, protecting communities with confidence.
Intuitive User Interface
RISE user-friendly interface is designed to make complex and essential data accessible to everyone. Both technical and non-technical experts can navigate the platform effortlessly. RISE minimizes the time spent learning the system, allowing users to focus on what really matters.
Historical Data Analysis
RISE transforms historical satellite and environmental data into strategic decisions for the future. Organizations can identify long-term patterns by examining past environmental and satellite data, identify recurring risks, and anticipate potential scenarios. This capability supports smarter planning and resilience strategies, ensuring that lessons from the past strengthen tomorrow's response.
Impacts Evaluation
Our platform offers automated impact assessments that reveal exactly what is at risk after an event. From roads and buildings to populations, crops, and critical infrastructure, RISE transforms complex information into clear insights. A dynamic dashboard brings everything together, percentages, affected areas, and risk comparisons, giving organizations the confidence to act quickly and effectively.
Multi-language
RISE was developed for global collaboration. With multilingual support, the platform adapts to various languages, ensuring accessibility and inclusion beyond geographical borders. International teams and humanitarian actors can collaborate easily, regardless of their location.
Tailored User Profiles
Each user is unique, and RISE reflects this with personalized profiles. The system adapts dashboards and settings based on roles, responsibilities, and preferences, ensuring that each user sees the most relevant information. Personalized experiences increase efficiency, reduce clutter, and allow individuals to focus on what matters most.
